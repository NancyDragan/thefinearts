Band

Ryan Foret teaches the band here at McNairy Central High School. The band is a program you can join any year. They have a band camp to help the kids get ready for the upcoming season of band. The band has Marching Season, Competition Season, and Concert Season.

During the finals and competition Mr. Foret can be very strict. The finals for band are very hard and last a long time, but at least the Bobcat Band got to go. They are 4th over all in the state. After finals Mr. Foret starts working on Concert Season.
