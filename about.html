About Our Fine Arts Program

The choir does two huge shows every year, Spring Show and the Christmas program.

The band is 4th over all in state. They go to competitions every year, and they almost always make a high placement.

The theater does small performances every now and then.

Both teachers work together at the Christmas program and Spring Show to make sure it's perfect. The teachers try not to over work their students, as they want their students to stay healthy.
