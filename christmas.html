Upcoming Christmas Events.

Every year the band and choir hold christmas events.

The choir has a Christmas concert on Dec 17th, at 7:00pm, in MCHS Little Theater. 3 to 5 dollars a ticket. They also get a party in class. For the Christmas concert the choir groups will be singing classic Christmas songs.

The band holds a Christmas parade. They also get a Christmas party, but normally after school or on weekends. The band is hosting 'Supper With The Clauses' on Saturday, Nov. 30th. Supper begins at 4 pm, followed by dessert and a story until 6 pm. Entrance fee is $10 and includes supper, dessert and a photo with Santa. Supper options provided by MaMaFia's.
