Theater

The theater here at our school is pretty neat. The kids get to write screen plays, help create costumes, build the set, and they can even be stage hands. Although theater class isn't all that easy, it is very fun and enjoyable. It tests your acting skills, make-up skills, and buliding stage props skills. With theater it can be very confusing, you have to set up for Spring Show, pratice, and still be able to do school work. That may seem hard, but Mr. Foret helps his students put on one great preformance.
